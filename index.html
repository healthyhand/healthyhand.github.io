COVID-19 Lifespan

Air

Lasts for 3 hours in air.

Cardboard

Lasts for 24 hours on cardboard.

Stainless Steel

Lasts for 2-3 days on stainless steel.

Plastic

Lasts for 3 days on plastic.

Source: New England Journal of Medicine

This means that one person with Covid-19 can open a door, and it could touch everyone else who also opens that door for the next three days.
