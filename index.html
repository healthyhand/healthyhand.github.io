We aim to help businesses feel more confident opening and to keep nursing homes safe with the Healthy Handle

The Product

We need stop the spread of COVID-19, now more than ever. Door handles are a germ hot spot ... but it's almost impossible for businesses to clean them after every user. Door handles are an unavoidable point of contact for many businesses and COVID-19 can remain on stainless steal surfaces for up to three days!

Thats why we made Healthy Handle! It is designed to eliminate the need for any establishment to manually clean their door handles on a regular basis.
