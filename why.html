Why Healhty Handle?

The affordable, easy-to-use door handle disinfects itself with every single use!

During the current COVID-19 Pandemic, we all need to put in every effort we can to stop the spread of COVID-19 and other infectious diseases more than ever. Door handles are a germ hot spot. You have to hold a door handle to enter and exit most buildings and COVID-19 can remain on stainless steal surfaces for up to three days! Since door handles are difficult to constntaly clean by hand, Healthy Handle is designed to eliminate the need for any establishment to manually clean their door handles on a regular basis.
